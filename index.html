Welcome to CHMDD

Cyanotoxins are toxic compounds produced by cyanobacteria, posing significant threats to aquatic ecosystems and human health. Although cyanotoxins are difficult to degrade under natural conditions, certain specialized microorganisms can effectively break down these toxins through metabolic activity. To advance research and applications in cyanotoxin degradation, we have developed this database, which systematically compiles the biodegradation relationships between microorganisms and cyanotoxins, as well as predictions of the underlying degradation mechanisms. We hope this database will provide valuable data support for researchers, helping to accelerate efforts to mitigate cyanotoxin contamination.
